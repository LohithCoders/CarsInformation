What Are Trucks?

Trucks are powerful vehicles designed for carrying heavy loads, towing, and off-road capabilities. They are highly versatile, making them ideal for both personal and professional use. Trucks are commonly used in construction, agriculture, and transportation industries, but they also appeal to those who need a reliable and durable vehicle for tough tasks.

Features of Trucks

  • High payload and towing capacity
  • Rugged and durable build
  • Available in various sizes (light-duty, medium-duty, heavy-duty)
  • Off-road capability for rough terrains
  • Spacious interiors and modern features for comfort
